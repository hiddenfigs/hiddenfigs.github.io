Ngadiuba is a leader in applying machine learning to particle physics: she co-develops tools that deploy deep neural networks onto FPGA hardware for ultra-fast, real-time inference in collider trigger systems, and she pioneers model-agnostic 'anomaly detection' searches for physics beyond the Standard Model.
